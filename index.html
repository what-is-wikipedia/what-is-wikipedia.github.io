What is Wikipedia



Get Started Here

Wikipedia is a popular free online knowledge encyclopaedia project website that is collaboratively edited by contributors from around the world. The word Wikipedia is made up of two words: "wiki" and "encyclopaedia." It contains articles on many topics.




How to use Wikipedia?


Here are the very easy steps, by following which you can easily sign in on YouTube and create your own channel on YouTube.
  1. Go to the official website by typing "wikipedia.org" in the address bar of your web browser.
  2. Enter the topic you want to know about in the search bar at the top right corner of the Wikipedia home page.
  3. Click on the magnifying glass icon or press "Enter" to start the search.
  4. Review the search results and select the article that best matches your desired topic.
  5. Be sure to click on the title of the article to access the full page.
      6. Wikipedia offers additional features such as language translation, printable versions of articles, discussion pages, and more. Take advantage of these features to enhance your experience.

Some simple rules for editing Wikipedia


  1. Create an account registered.
  2. Learn the five pillars.
  3. Be bold, but not reckless.
  4. Know your audience.
  5. Do not infringe copyright.
  6. Cite, Cite, Cite.
  7. Avoid self-promotion (shameless or otherwise) .
  8. Share your expertise, but don't argue with authority.
  9. Write in a neutral tone and with proper weight.
  10. Ask for help.
